<p><strong>The Online Streaming Platform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SVOD (Subscription-based Video on Demand)</li><li>TVOD (Transactional-based Video on Demand)</li><li>AVOD (Advertisement-based Video on Demand)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The online streaming platform market comprises three primary models: SVOD, TVOD, and AVOD. SVOD (Subscription-based Video on Demand) offers unlimited access to content for a recurring fee. TVOD (Transactional-based Video on Demand) allows users to pay for specific pieces of content, such as rentals or purchases. AVOD (Advertisement-based Video on Demand) provides free access to content, funded by advertisements interspersed throughout the viewing experience. Each model caters to different consumer preferences and monetization strategies.</p></p>
