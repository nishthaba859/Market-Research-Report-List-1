<p><p>The Cervical Total Disc Replacement Device market features two main types: Metal On Biocompatible Material (M-O-B) and Metal On Metal (M-O-M). M-O-B devices incorporate a metal component paired with a biocompatible material, promoting better integration with the body while reducing complications. In contrast, M-O-M devices consist solely of metal components, offering durability and strength but potentially higher wear rates. Both types aim to preserve mobility and alleviate pain in patients with cervical spine disorders, each with distinct advantages and considerations.</p></p>
