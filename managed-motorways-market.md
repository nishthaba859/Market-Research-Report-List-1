<p><strong>The Managed Motorways Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Controlled Motorways</li><li>Dynamic Hard Shoulder Running Schemes</li><li>All Lane Running Schemes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Managed Motorways enhance traffic flow and safety through various market types. Controlled Motorways utilize variable speed limits and lane signals to improve traffic conditions. Dynamic Hard Shoulder Running Schemes allow the hard shoulder to serve as an active lane during peak times, optimizing road use. All Lane Running Schemes remove the hard shoulder entirely, designating all lanes for general traffic, which increases capacity and enhances journey reliability. Each type aims to reduce congestion and improve overall motorway efficiency.</p></p>
